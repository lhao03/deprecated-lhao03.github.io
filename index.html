What am I doing?

I'm a 2nd year 👩🏻‍💻 Computer Science major and (hopeful) 🧬 Biochemistry minor at the University of British Columbia. Additionally, I'm an undergraduate teaching assistant for CPSC 210.

Currently, I am on a work term! I'm a software developer intern at The Centre of Excellence for Prevention of Organ Failure, a biotechnology company. Outside of school and work, I co-lead the software development of M2M, a project under UBC's Biomedical Engineering Student Team. M2M is an emerging mobile app that aims to gamify therapy and rehabilitation for those with multiple sclerosis. Additionally, I am the Director of Outreach and Technology at StarHacks, a hackathon that is now a nonprofit striving to create spaces for gender minorities in computer science.
